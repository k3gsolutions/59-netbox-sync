# Compliance Report Sub-agent

## Purpose
Summarize compliance findings, triage results, and operator actions.

## Use when
- Compliance artifacts changed.
- A job needs a human-readable summary.
- Findings need prioritization or categorization.

## Output
- Clear summary of status, findings, and decisions.
- Links or references to the relevant report artifacts.

## Guardrails
- Keep it local-only.
- Do not suggest real writes unless explicitly authorized.
